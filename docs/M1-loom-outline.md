# M1 Loom Outline

**For:** Jenni Dunman, after the Thursday demo
**Length:** about 4 minutes
**Format:** screen recording with Chris narrating, no webcam needed

This is a tighter version of the live demo. The live demo is for conversation, this Loom is so Jenni has something to rewatch on her own time, or share with Emma if she wants to.

The 4-minute target is firm. Anything longer than that and it stops being something Jenni will watch start to finish.

---

## Recording tips for Chris

A few practical notes before you press record.

- Use Loom in clean mode. No comments overlay, no notification badges, no Slack pinging in the background. Quit Slack and silence the phone.
- Sign in to the platform before you press record. The dashboard should already be on screen when the recording starts. Don't make Jenni watch you log in, the magic is the dashboard appearing.
- Have one franchisee detail page (Sarah, or whichever name shows up in the seed) pre-loaded in a second tab. When you click "Franchisees" in the recording you can flip to it instantly without scrolling.
- Don't sign out at the end. Cut the recording the moment you reach the closing line. Loom lets you trim, use it.
- If you stutter or fumble, re-record. Loom replaces the URL on a re-record so the link you send Jenni after the call stays the same. There's no penalty for retaking it.
- One pass at 1.0x speed should land at 4 minutes. If you're hitting 5 or 6, you're explaining too much. Cut the philosophy, keep the clicks.

## The script

Each block has the timestamp, what's on screen, and the line to say. Aim to keep transitions snappy, no more than half a second between clicks.

### [0:00–0:15] Login screen

**On screen:** `https://daisy-crm-platform.netlify.app` login page, before signing in.

**Say:**
> "This is `bookings.daisyfirstaid.com` once we're live. For now while we're still building it lives at `daisy-crm-platform.netlify.app`. Same login screen for HQ and franchisees, the system works out who you are when you sign in. Let me sign in as you."

Click into the email field, fill in, sign in.

---

### [0:15–0:50] HQ Dashboard

**On screen:** `/hq/dashboard` with four KPI cards, attention panel, activity feed.

**Say:**
> "And you're in. Four numbers up top: bookings this month, network revenue this month, active franchisees, territory coverage. The four things you've said you want to see Monday morning."

> "Below that, an attention panel, anything that needs you today. Overdue fees, quiet territories, new enquiries. Click any line and it takes you straight to the page that sorts it. Below that, an activity feed showing every recent change anyone's made."

---

### [0:50–1:25] Franchisees

**On screen:** Click "Franchisees" in the top nav, land on `/hq/franchisees`. Then click into a pre-loaded franchisee.

**Say:**
> "Every franchisee in one list. Search, sort, filter. Click any of them and you see everything about them on one page: their territories, every course they've run, every booking, every change anyone's made to their record. No more cross-referencing three systems to know how someone is doing."

---

### [1:25–1:55] Territory map

**On screen:** Click "Territories" in the top nav, land on `/hq/territories` with map.

**Say:**
> "Your network on a map. Green is active, amber is quiet, red is vacant. Click an empty territory and you can assign a franchisee from here. The country's coverage at a glance, no spreadsheet needed."

---

### [1:55–2:25] Bookings

**On screen:** Click "Bookings", land on `/hq/bookings`.

**Say:**
> "Every booking the network has taken. Filter by date, by payment status, by franchisee. Search a customer name, find their booking in two clicks, see the full audit on the detail page."

---

### [2:25–2:55] Reports

**On screen:** Click "Reports", land on `/hq/reports` with the revenue chart.

**Say:**
> "Revenue at a glance. Last six months, network-wide, plus a per-franchisee breakdown for the period. The numbers are live from the booking data, you don't have to add anything up by hand."

---

### [2:55–3:25] Billing preview

**On screen:** Click "Billing", click "Preview next run", select a franchisee with two territories, click Preview.

**Say:**
> "End-of-month fees. Pick a franchisee, pick a month, hit preview. The system calculates per territory, base fee or 10% whichever is higher, and shows you the working. From Phase 2 this runs automatically on each franchisee's billing date and pulls the money via direct debit."

---

### [3:25–3:50] Activity log

**On screen:** Click "Activity", land on `/hq/activity`.

**Say:**
> "Every change anyone makes, HQ or franchisee, gets logged here. Audit trail for free. If you ever need to know who did what when, it's on this page."

---

### [3:50–4:00] Closing

**On screen:** Stay on activity log, or flip back to dashboard.

**Say:**
> "That's M1. Nine clicks, the whole HQ side, everything you need in one place. Franchisee portal starts next week."

End recording.
